Waiting for geolocation...

Crikit

What did we do before thermometers? In the late 1800's two men independently published equations which tied temperature to the frequency of a cricket's chirps. Crikit uses geolocate data and the OpenWeatherMap API to get the temperature at the user's location. It then generates a chirping noise consistent with that temperature. Crikit seeks to remind us of what we have lost by distancing ourselves from the natural world.
